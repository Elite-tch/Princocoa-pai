Questions Answered

Everything
You Need To Know

How does the process work?

The journey begins with a discovery call where we understand your goals, current positioning, and where you want to be. You then choose the services that align with your needs—either starting with brand strategy, content production, or both simultaneously.

Which service should I start with?

We recommend beginning with the Personal Brand Identity Consultation. A clear brand strategy, defined messaging, and visual identity provide the foundation that makes your content production significantly more effective and intentional.

Do I need scripts for the content shoot?

You do not need pre-written scripts. Our optional Script Writing Assistance + Prompts add-on (₦60,000) provides structured talking points and narrative frameworks before your shoot so you arrive confident and prepared.

Can you help with content ideas?

Yes. Content idea development is embedded into the brand strategy process through Thought Leadership Pillars and Content Ecosystem Planning. For shoot-day content, the Script Writing add-on includes tailored prompts drawn from your expertise, audience, and positioning.

What should I wear to the shoot?

The Image Consulting module addresses wardrobe direction specifically. You will receive guidance on wardrobe choices that align with your brand positioning, on-camera presence, and the environments being used.

Can my team attend the shoot?

Yes. A small team presence can be arranged. We recommend keeping the environment focused on shoot day to maximise output quality and your on-camera energy. Specific arrangements are discussed during pre-shoot planning.

How long is the content shoot?

The shoot is designed to produce 30 days of content in a single production day. The exact duration depends on your package, locations, and preparation. Preparation guidance is provided in advance to ensure the day runs efficiently.

How quickly will I receive my deliverables?

Delivery timelines are confirmed during booking and vary by service and package. Raw footage is delivered faster than fully edited packages. Brand strategy deliverables are completed across your consultation sessions.

Can I book only one service?

Absolutely. All services are available as standalone offerings. Many clients begin with one and return for others as their visibility journey progresses.

Which content production package is best for me?

If you have an in-house editor, Raw Footage is cost-effective. If you want content immediately ready to publish, Fully Edited is the better investment. Premium Cinema is recommended for leaders who want a distinctly cinematic visual presence.

Do you travel for shoots and sessions?

Yes. Travel arrangements can be made for clients outside our base location. Travel-related costs are discussed transparently during the discovery call.

Can I book media placement separately?

Media placement is available as a standalone service within Growth & Visibility Services. Pricing is upon request as it is tailored to your goals, target media outlets, industry, and timeline.

How does newsletter management work?

Our newsletter management covers content planning, writing, design, scheduling, and delivery. You share your insights; we handle the execution. Pricing is upon request based on frequency, audience size, and requirements.

What happens after I book?

After booking you will receive a welcome communication with onboarding materials, intake questionnaire, and scheduling information. Brand strategy clients begin with a deep discovery session. Content production clients receive a pre-shoot planning call.

What industries do you work with?

We work with leaders across all industries—finance, technology, healthcare, faith, government, consulting, education, entertainment, real estate, and beyond. Our process is designed around your unique expertise and audience.
